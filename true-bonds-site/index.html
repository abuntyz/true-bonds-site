What makes friendship bracelets useful?

Friendship bracelets are small, but they carry a big message: you matter to me.

  • Reminds friends of each other every day
  • Helps friends feel close, even when far apart
  • Makes friends feel special and cared for
  • Shows that a friendship is strong and important
  • Helps friends remember fun times they shared
  • Shows that true friends always stay connected

What makes friendship bracelets special?

  • Uniquely crafted tokens of appreciation
  • Carefully crafted from embroidery floss with detailed knotting techniques
  • Symbolize an unbreakable bond between friends
  • Nurtures lasting friendships

Why we are making this

We are Sanvika and Vedansha. For our school project we wanted to share something positive: handmade jewelry that celebrates friendship. We learned how to plan costs, think about profit, compare ideas to other brands, and explain why our bracelets matter. True Bonds is our way of spreading kindness—one bracelet at a time.
